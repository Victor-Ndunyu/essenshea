# Essenshea brand direction

## Brand essence
Essenshea is a premium natural beauty brand rooted in ritual, warmth and botanical depth. The identity draws from the circular form of the logo, the organic shape of a leaf, and the grounded luxury of shea-based skincare.

## Visual language
- Tone: calm, elevated, sensual, and warm
- Color palette:
  - Cocoa brown: #1b1713
  - Golden sand: #8f6438
  - Forest green: #5d7346
  - Cream: #f7efe6
- Typography:
  - Display: Cormorant Garamond
  - Body: Inter
- Shape language:
  - Rounded geometry with subtle symmetry
  - Organic leaf-inspired accents
  - Soft shadows and tactile surfaces

## Messaging pillars
- Natural luxury
- Botanical ritual
- Retail and wholesale flexibility
- Giftable self-care
- Bespoke blends and elevated essentials

## Website direction
- Hero section with rich editorial imagery and a premium product-led focus
- Category cards that reflect the product catalogue
- Clear contact and wholesale conversion paths
- Minimal, high-end layouts with warm neutrals and service-first messaging

## Motion direction
- Slow fades and gentle hover transitions
- Soft card elevation rather than flashy effects
- A calm, luxurious feel that mirrors the brand
