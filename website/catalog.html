How to shop

Request-based ordering, made simple.

Essenshea operates on a request model — not a traditional checkout. Here is how it works:

  1. Browse our catalog or shop page and find what you need.
  2. Add items to your request cart. Some products are available now; others are made to order.
  3. Submit your request and we will confirm pricing, availability and a fulfilment schedule within 24 hours.
  4. We handle the rest — whether it is a single jar or a wholesale pallet.
