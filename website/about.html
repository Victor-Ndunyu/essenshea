The Essenshea story

Crafted for people who want beauty that feels both purposeful and indulgent.

Essenshea brings together organic shea, nourishing oils, fragrance-led body care and wellness essentials into a grounded luxury experience for everyday ritual.

Our philosophy

From whips and balms to aromatic oils and sacred scents, the collections are crafted around botanical nourishment and the comfort of tactile self-care. Whether it is a daily body oil, a custom blend, or a gift set for someone special, every product is designed to feel considered, elevated and personal.

Essenshea supports flexible purchasing for individual customers, retail shelves and curated bulk orders with a direct, responsive service model. We believe that luxury should not be hurried — and that the best beauty rituals are the ones that ask you to slow down.

What we stand for

Principles that shape every product.

Rooted in nature

From wild-harvested shea to cold-pressed oils, every ingredient is chosen for its purity and purpose. We work with the earth, not against it.

Made for modern rituals

Our products are designed to fit seamlessly into your daily practice — whether that is a morning oil massage, a nighttime body butter ritual, or a fragrant pause in the middle of a busy day.

Crafted, not mass-produced

Each batch is made with attention to detail. We do not chase volume; we chase quality. This is why every jar, bottle and set feels distinct.

Built for every scale

Whether you are ordering a single body butter or stocking a retail shelf, you receive the same product quality and the same personal service. Wholesale, retail and custom formulations all follow the same standard: excellence.

Present and unhurried

We do not believe in rush orders or impersonal transactions. Every inquiry is met with warmth, every request is handled with care, and every product is shipped only when it is ready.
